General Information

Date and location

The school will be held from 16 to 20 September 2024 at the Institute of Archaeology, Czech Academy of Sciences, Brno (https://arub.cz/) at the address given below:

Čechyňská 363/19
602 00 Brno
Czech Republic

Note

Please note that this is an on-site event only, and remote participation will not be possible.

Grants

Each participant of the school is eligible to receive a grant to cover travel expenses, housing, and daily subsistence during the meeting. To receive the grant, participants need to:

  1. Apply via the official form of the ATRIUM Project (see the Applications section for details).
  2. Participate in person, i.e. sign attendance sheets for each training day.
  3. Submit a report within a given time frame after the conclusion of the school with details of their participation.

Prerequisites

  • Bring your own laptop!
  • Install R and RStudio (follow the concise quide here).
    If you are familiar with R, feel free to use a GUI of your choice.

Other information

Travelling to Brno

Brno is easily reachable by trains or buses from Prague (approx. a 2 to 2.5-hour journey) and Vienna (approx. a 1.5 to 2-hour journey). Many overnight trains crossing a large part of Europe start or end in Vienna, and we recommend taking these trains as an alternative to flying.
